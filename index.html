Overview

This story moves through three Seattle places. As you scroll, the map will fly to each location and show a popup.

Downtown / Pike Place

Downtown Seattle has long been a center of tourism and commerce, and the waterfront area continues to change with redevelopment and public projects.

University of Washington

The U-District reflects the impact of UW through high student housing demand, transit, and constant neighborhood development.

Rainier Valley (Columbia City)

Rainier Valley is known for cultural diversity and neighborhood identity, while also experiencing growth and change along major transit developments.

Ending

These three places show how different parts of Seattle experience change in different ways which are through tourism, institutions, and community identity.
